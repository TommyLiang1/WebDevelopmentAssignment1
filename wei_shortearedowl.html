Short Eared Owl

Symbolism: Short-eared Owls are active in the daytime. Some Native American tribes such as the Cherokee, Lakota, and Caddo believed that owls may bring news to medicine men who are able to understand them. This news may tell of events happening at a far away distance or of events of the future.
